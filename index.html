Why One-Click Tech Transfer Matters Now

Tech transfer is still measured in months because knowledge remains fragmented, manual, and difficult to reuse.​

Despite increasing digitalization, critical transfer knowledge remains scattered across documents, systems, and expert teams. One Click Tech Transfer transforms this fragmented knowledge into a structured, reviewable, and compliant foundation for faster decision-making and transfer execution.​

Merck already has strong foundations, the challenge is connecting them into reusable tech-transfer decisions.​

Merck has invested significantly in process knowledge, semantic foundations, and manufacturing expertise. Yet critical transfer knowledge remains spread across documents, systems, and expert teams, creating manual effort and limiting reuse across transfers.​

CURRENT STATE​

Fragmented knowledge​​

Process knowledge exists across documents, systems, and people but lacks a unified structure for reuse.

Semantic-to-Document Gap

Valuable semantic models exist but are not yet directly connected to the document-centric transfer workflow.

Manual Process Comparison

Experts still compare process parameters and receiving-site capabilities manually.

PCS creation

Process Control Strategies, work instructions, and manufacturing scenarios require significant manual drafting effort.​

Manual Downstream Preparation

eBR, MES, DeltaV, and PAS-X inputs must still be prepared manually.

TARGET STATE​

Connected Knowledge

A governed Knowledge Twin unifies process, quality, and regulatory knowledge into one structured, reusable source across every transfer.

Automated Assessment

The platform automatically compares process needs against receiving-site equipment and constraints, surfacing gaps and risks early.

AI-Assisted, Human-in-the-Loop Generation

AI drafts PCS, work instructions, and manufacturing scenarios from governed knowledge; SMEs review, correct, and approve every output.

Structured Outputs

eBR, MES, DeltaV, and PAS-X inputs are generated from approved knowledge and decisions, ready for system use.

Digitally Upskill Employees

Freed from manual compilation, transfer teams build digital skills and set the benchmark for future ways of working.

Built on Merck's target architecture, not alongside it.

The solution extends Merck's existing technology direction, but the transformation is broader than technology alone. It's built across people, process, and technology together:

  • Technology: Semantic foundations, agent orchestration, user-centric workflows, and embedded governance combine into a single operating model. 

  • Process: Tech Transfer moves from a series of disconnected, manually-reconciled steps to one governed workflow. 

  • People: It's a genuine digital upskilling opportunity that gives transfer teams access to future-ready ways of working, with time freed from manual compilation to focus on review, judgment, and higher-value decisions.
